VERDURE DESIGN COMPETITION'14

Have you ever envisioned the perfect villa???? Here’s your chance to breathe life into your dream project. Excel 2014 in association with Nucleus Properties is providing this unprecedented platform to all designers out there. The goal is to create a project on waterfront development of villa in approximately two acres. We welcome new typologies in sustainability and design that answers the context and program in innovative ways. The best designer and the runner up shall bag a bounty of 25000 and 10000 INR each. Internship offers at Nucleus Properties are at stake here for the 5 best performers. So..what are you waiting for???!!! Grab your sketchbooks today.

Rules And Regulations

  1. Eligibility: All students with a valid student id card from their respective colleges are allowed to participate.

  2. Team Specifications: You can enter the contest individually or as a team.A maximum of 2 members are allowed in a team.

  3. a. Procedure: Prepare an abstract of your dream villa. Make sure it contains PLOT DISTRIBUTION & LAYOUT OF ONE TOPOLOGY (any one unit villa). Send your abstracts to verdure@excelmec.org. Last date for submission is 21st September 2014.

    b. Final round will be conducted on 26th September 2014. Be prepared for a presentation with 3D designs.

What we’re looking for

1.Sensitivity to site ecology and natural habitat.

2.  Walkability / alternative modes of transportation.

3. Passive design.

4. Net-zero annual site energy use.

5. Efficient on-site water management strategies.

6.  Use of healthy and environmentally-responsible building materials.

7. Natural ventilation and indoor air quality.

8. Access to daylight and views.

9. Access to public open space.

10.  Opportunities for recreation and physical activity.

11. Access to fresh, local foods and agriculture opportunities.
