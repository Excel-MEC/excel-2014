Event Format

  1. The event will be a team event with a team of 3 or 4.
  2. The event has total 3 rounds, where the first round is a prelims and the third round is the final.
  3. We use a simple and attractive card based system to decided the finalists. One of three coloured cards with different weights will be awarded on successful completion of each task.


Round 1

  1. Some basic electronics questions to test your knowledge and thinking abilities.
  2. Only the worthy pass through.


Round 2

  1. You can do it as a team itself.
  2. Some basic tasks to test the skills at an application level.
  3. The top four teams go into the final round.


Round 3

  1. The final round! Your ability to apply your knowledge in a real world scenario is tested.
  2. All your skills as an engineer is tested here.
  3. Do you call yourself an engineer?? Come and prove it!!
  4. Find out the rest for yourself by coming to EXCEL 2014 ;)

Detailed Rules And Regulations

  1. The event will be a team event with each team comprising of three or four members.

  2. All the members of a team need NOT be from the same college.

  3. College ID cards are mandatory at the time of participation.

  4. All the required components and datasheets will be provided. NO external components are allowed.

  5. Use of mobile phones during the event is not allowed.

  6. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

  7. Any team that does not abide by the rules will be suspended immediately.

  8. The rules are subject to change. The rules will be explained before the start of the event as well.
