Webbots
Prize Money: ₹ 15K+

We've all played the game 'Snake' haven't we? Guiding a snake on a feeding frenzy as it grows with every bite.

Web bots takes the classic 'Snake' to a whole new level - You are the designer and creator of your snake's AI. You code your serpent to lead it on the path of safety. But wait!! There's a twist. Your's is not the only hungry reptile around. Gear yourself up as you compete with a hostile opponent snake in the same arena. Contestants pit their bots against each other to see who survive the longest without hitting themselves or their opponents.

Robowars using computers!!!

Event Format

Web Bots will be an online multi-level game that tests your skill over several levels spread across three weeks. Players are to code an AI bot for the game. (Don't worry, we've made it easy to write a bot). These bots are pitted against each other and the bot that wins, advances to the next round. The bot who wins all the rounds, bags the prize.

The game is simple. Bots are two colored cells on an NxN grid. Bots leave a colored trail wherever they move. A bot must not hit its own trail or that of another or the walls. Got it? Those are the bots that you'll be coding.

Rules And Regulations

  1. Each player can enter only one bot for the final tournament, although you could modify the code for your bot, your final saved code would be used for the competition.
  2. Players would be disqualified if they have been found to tamper with the site or the system.
  3. Rules are subject to change at any point in time.
  4. The decision of the Web-Bots team would be final.

In-Game rules:

  1. Eating a fruit increases the size of the snake.
  2. The scores will be updated after every match
  3. The game comes to an end under any of the following conditions :
  1. Your Snake hits the opponent's Snake.
  2. The Snake hits any of the four walls.
  3. The Snake hits itself.
