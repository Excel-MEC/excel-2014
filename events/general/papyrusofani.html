Event Format

There will be two phases for the event. The initial phase is the online submission of the abstract. The abstracts will be verified by a panel and the best ones will be shortlisted for the next phase. The final phase is the presentation which will be conducted at EXCEL 2014 at the time allotted for each category. There will be 4 categories under which participants can compete. They include:

  1. Electronics and Communication and Biomedical Engineering.

  2. Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

  3. Computer science Engineering.

  4. General (papers from mechanical, civil or non technical papers related to physics, maths etc.)

The participants will be judged by a panel for each category.

Rules And Regulations

Team Size - 3 members (maximum)
Rules are subject to change at any point in time. All rules will be explained at the event as well.


Phase I
1.  Submission of abstract on or before 10/9/2014 to poa@excelmec.org
2.  Abstract format will be available in the website.
3.  After evaluation, the selected candidates will be informed via email on or before 18th September.
4.  The number of participants in each team should not exceed 3.
5.  Any paper that does not fall into the category of topics (suggested below), but are innovative and original will also be encouraged.
6.  A team cannot send more than 2 entries.
7.  Only students pursuing their graduation and post-graduation can participate.


Phase II
1.  Only the submitted topics can be presented.
2.  3 hard copies of the final paper have to be submitted in IEEE format.
3.  Presentations have to be in PowerPoint format only and the number of slides should not exceed 25.
4.  Total time allotted for presentation is 15 minutes: 12 minute presentation+3 minute interaction.
5.  Medium of presentation must be in English.
6.  Decision of judges will be final and binding.
7.  Excel 2014 team reserves the right to take decisions in case of any disputes.
