Defacto
Prize Money: ₹ 11K+

“Any fool can know. The point is to understand.” ― Albert Einstein

Defacto was born out of Excel's ambition to reach out the young Einsteins and Madam Curies in school. A quiz aimed solely at school students, Defacto promises to be a fun and interactive session, combining the innovation of college quizzing and the inquisitiveness of school quizzing.

Let your intellect shine, let the rolling of brain race with every passing of minute and get yourselves ready for a gem of a quiz!

Event Format

Stage 1- Prelims

A 25 questions written round aimed at selecting the top 6 two-member teams.

Finals

Consisting of 6 rounds of audiovisual and text based questions.

Rules and Regulations

  1. School students from classes 7-12 can participate.

  2. A team should comprise of 2 members.

  3. Cross school teams allowed.

  4. Online registration and spot registration available for the teams. Spot registration will be closed half an hour before the prelims.

  5. School id cards is mandatory.

  6. Winners and runners up of the quiz will be rewarded.

  7. The team with the most innovative ‘name’ will be rewarded.

  8. The school with the maximum participation will also be rewarded.

  9. Decision of the quizmaster will be final and binding.
