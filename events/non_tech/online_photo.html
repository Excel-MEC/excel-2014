Third Eye Photography
Prize Money: ₹ 20K+

For all those shutterbugs who believe that your favourite personal photograph is the one you are going to capture next. Amateur photographers are encouraged to think on their feet and come up with interesting snapshots. As the title suggests-speed matters. So, if you tend to ponder much, before turning on those creative juices, maybe this is not your cup of tea.

Event Format

The event will have 2 rounds - Online Photography and Instant Photography.

The online round will contain 4 sessions. Each session for a week starts on Thursday and end the following Tuesday. Each session will have different themes which will be updated on the official facebook page on every Wednesday. The participants can mail images a maximum of 3 in one session. The participant can participate in all the four sessions. According to the theme they have to mail their photos to our mail ID. All the images received will be uploaded in Excel’s Facebook, and Google+ pages, in an album, ‘Third Eye - Online Photography Entries’ with the caption submitted by the participant along with the ID no. provided to each participant and on Instagram, with the hashtags provided along with the entry. There will be a point system(MegaPixels system) as given below.

Points System (MegaPixels) :
Participation in each round : 1MP
First in one session : 10MP
Second in one session : 5MP
Third in one session 2MP


Judging Criteria - ‘Judge's Choice’ winner will be selected by a panel of judges. The winner will be decided purely based on thought process behind the photo, quality and creativity.
‘People's Choice’ winner will be decided on the basis of the popularity of the photo (based only on LIKES and SHARES) ‘People's Choice’ winner will be valued as per the scoring system below -
Facebook like / Google+ +1 - 1 point
Facebook share / Google+ share - 2 points
Instagram likes - 2 points
The winners will be announced in the excel Mec Facebook Page.


How to participate?

  1. Send upto 3 photos (JPG format max 3MB) to thirdeye@excelmec.org
  2. Subject of the mail should be like this : Name-Age-College
  3. Body should contain your Name, Age, College, Branch, Place, Year, Caption 1, Caption 2, Caption 3, 10 HashTags for each entry on instagram.
  4. Attach upto 3 entries less than 3 MB in size.


Instant Round

Participants will be given various themes onsite and asked to take photographs based on the given themes. Participants can submit atmost 3 photographs based on the theme. Photographs are awarded marks based on the content, originality and their relevance to the theme. There will be 2 sessions without elimination and a final session for the top 5 in leaderboard.


Points System (MegaPixels) - Cumulative from Online Round

Participation in each session : 2MP
First in one session : 15MP
Second in one session : 10MP
Third in one session 3MP

Rules and Regulations

  1. Photographs using any type of cameras are allowed for the competition (DSLR, SLR, Mobile cameras) and mention them with entry.

  2. Participants should be below the age 25.

  3. A participant can have a maximum of 3 entries per week for a theme.

  4. All entries should contain a short description of the photo, the name of the participant, name of the college/organisation /school, phone number, if possible details like (Camera Model, lens used, Shutter speed, Aperture, ISO ).

  5. Presence of any sort of vulgarism, racism or provocation as well as copied entries, if found with valid proof, will be disqualified and deleted from the album immediately.

  6. The likes from ‘people’s choice ‘are expressed in MP (Mega Pixels), these points provide an edge for the participants in our instant photography event, the more likes and share you earn the more pixels you earn.

  7. Watermarks of any kind are NOT allowed. Submitted pictures will be watermarked from our side to avoid misuse of the submitted picture.

  8. Any use of unfair means in the form of plagiarism or show of popularity using unfair mechanisms will result in the photo being not considered for people's choice winner.

  9. Major editing of the photograph other than cropping or simple editing is not allowed.

  10. The photographs uploaded under a theme after the closing Tuesday will not be included in the competition.

  11. Upon winning, the participant should submit ID proof and age proof along with the original photograph.

  12. Rules are subject to change at any point in time.
